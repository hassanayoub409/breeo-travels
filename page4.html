WHY TURKEY?

AUTHOR : anonymous

Turkey is a developing country which has a massive tourist industry which is programmed in a way to provide all the necassery and sufficient services to the tourists. The people in Turkey are open-minded and accept foriegn tourist whole-heartedly.There are many beautiful places in Turkey which inculcate eternal memories on human mind.

Reasons for choosing turkey

Turkey’s nature is beautiful and perfect for hiking! I know few countries where the locals are so hospitable, helpful and kind. In addition, I absolutely love Turkish food! Mezze, clay pot (food prepared in a clay pot that is broken open when served), kebab, gözleme (filled flatbread), simit (bagels) and of course baklava. Even as a vegetarian there is always plenty of choices. I was also surprised at how well you can do Turkey on a budget! So be sure to read the budget blog below. Did you know that you can also ski in Turkey? There are quite high mountains in the country.
